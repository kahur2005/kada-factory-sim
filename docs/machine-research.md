Task 1 seeded every catalog entry's `throughput` block with an estimate. This pass
researched the ~15 flagged stations against vendor datasheets/spec pages via web
search, upgraded `rated`/`source`/`confidence` where a usable published figure
exists, and left the rest as estimates. No `unit` or `perPhone` value changed from
Task 1 — research did not contradict any of the component-count assumptions.

## Assumptions

- 1 mainboard per phone.
- ~800 passives + ~80 ICs per mainboard (drives `placer-hs`/`placer-fine` `perPhone`).
- Rates are per-station **sustained** rates, not burst/optimum figures — placer CPH datasheet numbers are derated 50–65% per the brief's guidance before being accepted.
- Logistics rates (`loader`, `conveyor`) are transport, not processing, and are excluded from the bottleneck calculation in `deriveMetrics`.
